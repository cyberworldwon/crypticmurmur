A manifesto of my style

black metal

raw sound without too much polishing. general dark themes. not for the aggression — for what lives underneath it.

gif art

early internet. a vibe of freedom. mostly what i reach for when making visuals for songs.

magic

i believe it exists but not in a way that's entirely comprehensible. i try to create an eerie feeling with sound. something that doesn't fully explain itself.

nature paintings

Tom Uttech

i love nature. visual art fascinates me partially because i can't master it. something i can only look at, never make.

cossack culture

my heritage. freedom again, but with a sense of belonging this time.

2010s beatmaking

the last real prime. these days beats sound copypasted and soulless. cloudrap was big during my teenage years and it got into me whether i wanted it to or not.
